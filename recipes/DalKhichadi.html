Dal - Khichadi

Description

Khichdi or khichri (Urdu: کھچڑی, romanized: khicṛī, Hindi: खिचड़ी, romanized: khicṛī, Bengali: খিচুড়ি, romanized: Khicuṛi, Odia: ଖେଚୁଡି) is a dish in South Asian cuisine made of rice and lentils (dal) with numerous variations.

Variations include bajra and mung dal khichri. In Indian culture, in several regions, especially in the northern areas, it is considered one of the first solid foods that babies eat.

Ingredients

  • Rice - Indrayani Preffered
  • Dal - Moong Preffered

Steps:

  1. Get the utensils cleaned
  2. Add two cups of rice into the cooker
  3. Add 4 cups of water
  4. Add 1 cup of Daal
  5. Some haldi, half tea spoon of salt
